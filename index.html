Retro style hoodie for ultimate comfort and style.

Description

The Felice is a hoodie with a retro feeling and unique detailing. This slightly cropped style is the ultimate casual sportswear hoodie. The wider fit, cropped waist and slightly shorter sleeve give this hoodie it’s unique feeling and it can be worn either casually at home or as a sportswear essential. It is the perfect layering piece and is a fashion twist to the classic hoodie.

Unique Features

The super soft, contrast colour flock print on the front of the garment gives this hoodie a gorgeous retro feeling. The detailing of the rubber cord ends and logo print on the sleeve add a flash of class and the unique pocket on the back of the style is perfect for tucking away your phone or money while training. We have tried to combine a fashion classic with essential sport detailing to give this style a rich and luxurious feeling.
